Cevapi

Best food ever a.k.a Cevapi!

Ćevapi are a beloved Croatian dish, often enjoyed as a street food or at family gatherings. These small, grilled sausages, typically made from a mixture of ground beef and pork, are seasoned with onion, garlic, and spices, giving them a rich flavor. Served in soft, fluffy bread called somun, they are often accompanied by chopped onions and red pepper-based condiments. Ćevapi embody the essence of Balkan cuisine, showcasing the region's culinary traditions and the joy of communal dining.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Olive oil (For grilling)

Step by step instructions:

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a bowl, combine beef, pork, onion, garlic, paprika, baking soda, salt, and peppers. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape Cevapi: Form the mixture into small, finger-sized sausages (about 10 cm long).
  3. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to firm up.
  4. Preheat Grill: Heat the grill or a skillet over medium-high heat
  5. Cook: Brush with olive oil and grill Ćevapi for 4-5 minutes on each side, until browned and cooked through.
  6. Serve: Enjoy with somun (bread) and onions, or with your favorite dips!
