Burek

Next best food a.k.a Burek!

Burek is a popular Croatian pastry, renowned for its flaky, golden layers of phyllo dough filled with savory fillings like minced meat, cheese, or spinach. Often enjoyed as a snack or main dish, it reflects the rich culinary heritage of the Balkans. Whether served at bakeries or home-cooked, burek is beloved for its delicious flavors and satisfying texture, making it a staple in Croatian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (or meat of your choice)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 300g phyllo dough
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 100ml water (for moisture)

Now lets get into making this dreamfood:

  1. Prepare Filling: In a pan, sauté onions in olive oil until soft. Add ground meat, salt, pepper, and paprika. Cook until browned. Let cool.
  2. Layer Phyllo: Lay out one sheet of phyllo dough, brush with olive oil, and layer another sheet on top. Repeat for 3-4 layers.
  3. Add Filling: Place a line of meat filling along one edge of the phyllo.
  4. Roll: Roll the phyllo over the filling to form a log. Coil it into a spiral shape if desired
  5. Bake: Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F). Place burek on a baking sheet, brush with beaten egg, and bake for 30-40 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Serve: Enjoy warm, often with yogurt or a side salad!
