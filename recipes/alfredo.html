Pasta Alfredo

The most delicious and tasty pasta you will ever experience!

A man named Alfredo di Lelio invented it. Di Lelio came up with this famous dish right here in Rome in 1908. Legend says that his wife had lost her appetite after giving birth, so he came up with this simple but delicious pasta recipe. Soon, it made an appearance on the menu at the family restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 250g Pasta Penne
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 garlic clovers
  • Seasoning of your liking
  • 300ml heavy whipping cream
  • The more parmesan the better

Lets get into making this awesome dish!

  1. First cook the spaghetti
  2. get a wok pan and set the temperature to medium high heat.
  3. After the pan is warm, add in your butter and garlic
  4. Let the garlic work it's magic for a minute or two then add your whipping cream
  5. Follow the whipping cream up with seasoning of your choice and the parmesan
  6. Once the sauce thickens add in your spaghetti and enjoy!
